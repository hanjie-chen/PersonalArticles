# Pitfall Guide to Zizhu Third Restaurant

Detailed address: Zizhu Third Restaurant, Zizhu High-Tech Zone, Minhang District, Shanghai

I have gone to this restaurant for meals with my colleague jiuqi many times, and these are some notes we have summarized from those visits.

The noodle stall is in a relatively hidden location, so you may need to look carefully or ask someone.

## Pork Oil-Splashed Noodles (Wide Noodles)

<img src="./resources/images/pork-noddles.jpg" alt="pork_noddles" style="zoom:45%;" />

> [!note]
>
> Note that the wide noodles are hand-pulled on the spot by the chef. Personally, I think they taste better.
>
> The thin noodles, however, are not made on the spot. They are ready-made, and feel more like something bought from a market.

## Beef Oil-Splashed Noodles

jiuqi said the taste was decent, but compared with its price of 27 CNY, the pork oil-splashed noodles are still better value.

I tried some. The beef is all lean meat, so it is actually a bit dry. It would taste better with a little fat. The pork is also all lean, but it is softer. Pork oil-splashed noodles are the GOAT.

<img src="./resources/images/beef-noddles.jpg" alt="beef_noddles" style="zoom:33%;" />

> [!note]
>
> Note that the peanuts are added separately and cost 2 CNY.

## Saozi Noodles (Thin Noodles)

This includes Qishan saozi noodles and dry-mixed saozi noodles. After trying them, neither jiuqi nor I recommend them.

## Roujiamo

jiuqi tried it and does not recommend it. The meat filling is chopped pork knuckle.

## Chicken Cutlet Rice

<img src="./resources/images/chicken-cutlet-rice.jpg" alt="rice" style="zoom:33%;" />

At the moment, I only recommend the chicken cutlet rice (18 CNY). It is affordable and tasty, but the only truly good part is the freshly fried chicken cutlet.

## Pork Tripe Chicken

I feel like only the chicken tastes decent. Everything else is average. At 30 CNY, the value is not even as good as the beef oil-splashed noodles.

The dry pot chicken wings also taste pretty good. It feels like the chicken quality at this restaurant is generally quite good.

<img src="./resources/images/pork-stomach-chicken.jpg" alt="pork tripe chicken" style="zoom:50%;" />

# Dry Pot

Price list:

<img src="./resources/images/dry-pot-menu.jpeg" alt="menu" style="zoom:30%;" />

## Beef Dry Pot

The taste is average, and how good it is depends on the side ingredients. Sometimes it comes with garlic scapes, which I do not like. Sometimes it comes with potatoes, onions, and green and red peppers, which tastes fairly decent.

<img src="./resources/images/dry-pot-beef.jpg" alt="beef" style="zoom:50%;" />

## Chicken Wing Dry Pot

It tastes good. The chicken is very fresh. Most pieces are drumettes, so there are a lot of bones. It is also quite salty.

<img src="./resources/images/dry-pot-chicken.jpeg" alt="chiken" style="zoom:25%;" />

The side ingredients can sometimes be rather plain, with only green peppers, but the taste is still the same.

<img src="./resources/images/dry-pot-chicken-green.jpg" alt="chicken-green pepper" style="zoom:50%;" />

## Bullfrog Dry Pot

I feel the taste is average and do not recommend it. The large pieces of bullfrog meat do not absorb much flavor. For the price of 28 CNY, it is still not very worthwhile.

<img src="./resources/images/frog-dry-pot.jpg" alt="frog" style="zoom:50%;" />

# Clay Pot

I feel like they all taste very average, and they all seem to have glass noodles? I do not really like glass noodles.

## Lamb Clay Pot

The lamb clay pot has far too strong a mutton smell. It is basically impossible to eat. Do not order it.

<img src="./resources/images/lamb-clay-pot.jpg" alt="lamp" style="zoom:30%;" />

# Fast Food Counter

I also do not know what this dish is called. After jiuqi tried it, it seemed to be minced meat wrapped in glutinous rice with salted egg yolk. In any case, jiuqi’s verdict was that it did not taste very good.

<img src="./resources/images/windows-food.jpg" alt="windows-food" style="zoom:50%;" />

The fried small chicken drumsticks are especially good, especially flavorful, especially fresh, and absolutely excellent.
